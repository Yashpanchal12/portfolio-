How Small Businesses Can Track Sales Using Power BI

Many small businesses still track sales using notebooks or basic Excel files. This creates confusion and delays in decision-making. Power BI helps businesses see sales, profit, and trends clearly in one dashboard.

The Problem with Traditional Sales Tracking

Manual tracking leads to errors, no real-time visibility, and unclear profit data. Business owners often rely on guesswork instead of facts.

How Power BI Solves This Problem

Power BI converts raw sales data into visual dashboards showing daily, monthly, and yearly performance with clear charts and KPIs.

My Process

  • Collect sales data (Excel / billing software)
  • Clean and organize data
  • Create Power BI dashboard
  • Add KPIs and automate updates

Business Benefits

Power BI saves time, improves decision-making, and gives complete control over business performance.
